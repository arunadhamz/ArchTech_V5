### 6.2 Traceability Purpose

Requirements traceability ensures:
- All requirements are implemented in the design
- Changes to requirements can be traced through the design
- Verification and validation can be performed systematically
- Compliance with standards and regulations
- Effective change management

### 6.3 Traceability Process

The traceability process includes:
- Requirement identification and categorization
- Design element mapping
- Implementation verification
- Change impact analysis
- Compliance verification
