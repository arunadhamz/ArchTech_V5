# SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS SPECIFICATION (SyRS) - MECHANICAL REQUIREMENTS

## 3.4 Mechanical Requirements Specification

Mechanical requirements define the physical characteristics and constraints of the system. Each requirement is identified with a unique identifier in the format `<BID/PID/SID>-<SyRS>-XXX`.

### Dimensional Requirements

Dimensional requirements specify the physical size constraints including:
- Overall system dimensions (Length x Width x Height)
- Weight limitations and targets
- Mounting envelope specifications
- Clearance requirements for maintenance and installation

### Mounting Requirements

Mounting requirements define how the system is secured including:
- Mounting hole specifications and locations
- Mounting hardware requirements
- Load-bearing capabilities
- Vibration and shock mounting considerations
- Thermal expansion considerations

### Material Specifications

Material specifications define the physical materials used including:
- Enclosure materials and finishes
- Corrosion resistance requirements
- Flammability ratings
- Conductivity specifications
- Surface finish requirements
- Environmental compliance specifications

### Thermal Management

Thermal management requirements include:
- Operating temperature ranges
- Storage temperature ranges
- Heat dissipation requirements
- Cooling method specifications (conduction, convection, active cooling)
- Thermal interface material requirements

### Connector and Interface Layout

Connector layout requirements specify:
- Physical locations of connectors
- Connector orientation and accessibility
- Cable management specifications
- Strain relief requirements
- Connector protection specifications

### Environmental Protection

Environmental protection requirements include:
- Ingress protection ratings (IP ratings)
- Sealing requirements for connectors and enclosures
- Corrosion protection specifications
- Shock and vibration resistance requirements
- Pressure differential specifications

### Manufacturing and Assembly

Manufacturing and assembly requirements include:
- Tolerance specifications
- Surface mount technology requirements
- Component accessibility for replacement
- Automated test equipment interface requirements
- Quality and inspection criteria

These mechanical requirements ensure the system meets all necessary physical constraints while maintaining reliability and performance in the target operational environment.
