# SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS SPECIFICATION (SyRS) - ENVIRONMENTAL REQUIREMENTS

## 3.5 Environmental Requirements Specification

Environmental requirements define the conditions under which the system must operate reliably. Each requirement is identified with a unique identifier in the format `<BID/PID/SID>-<SyRS>-XXX`.

### Temperature Requirements

Temperature requirements specify the operational and storage temperature ranges:
- Operating temperature range
- Storage temperature range
- Temperature cycling requirements
- Thermal shock specifications
- Rate of temperature change limitations

### Humidity Requirements

Humidity requirements specify:
- Operating humidity range
- Storage humidity range
- Humidity cycling specifications
- Condensation and moisture resistance

### Vibration and Shock Requirements

Vibration and shock requirements specify:
- Operational vibration specifications
- Transport and storage shock limits
- Random vibration profiles
- Sine vibration requirements
- Sustainment shock limits

### Altitude and Pressure Requirements

Altitude and pressure requirements specify:
- Operational altitude range
- Storage altitude range
- Pressure differential specifications
- Vacuum exposure requirements

### Contamination and Corrosion Requirements

Contamination and corrosion requirements specify:
- Dust and particulate resistance
- Salt spray resistance
- Chemical exposure specifications
- Fungal resistance requirements
- Flammability specifications

### Electromagnetic Environmental Requirements

Electromagnetic requirements specify:
- Electromagnetic interference (EMI) limits
- Electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) requirements
- Radio frequency (RF) immunity specifications
- Electrostatic discharge (ESD) resistance

### Power Quality Requirements

Power quality requirements specify:
- Input power variations
- Power interruption tolerance
- Voltage fluctuation limits
- Frequency variation tolerance

### Transportation and Handling Requirements

Transportation and handling requirements specify:
- Drop and impact specifications
- Transportation vibration profiles
- Packaging requirements
- Handling procedure constraints

### Storage Requirements

Storage requirements specify:
- Storage duration limitations
- Storage environment conditions
- Maintenance requirements during storage
- Pre-operational preparation procedures

These environmental requirements ensure the system can operate reliably in the intended operational environment while maintaining performance throughout its lifecycle.
